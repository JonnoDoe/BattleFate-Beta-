Barbarian

Barbarians are ignorant, rude, cruel people. They are good at axes. They prefer light armor, which does not hamper their movement in the battle with the enemy. Worship the god Tyr. Consider death in battle, the most dignified death. They prefer to rob, than to earn honest labor. The main characteristic of a barbarian is agility.

Class bonuses

When health falls below 20% of the maximum reserve, the physical damage dealt by the barbarian increases by 20%, and the any resulting damage decreases by 30%

Ruthless blow

Barbarian attacks his opponent with incredible force, inflicting damage on him in the amount of 150% of the physical attack rate.

Spends 15 MP

Battle rage

Barbarian gives vent to his anger, causing physical damage he deals to increase by 20% for three turns.

Spends 20 MP

Wizard

Wizards prefer to wear fabric robes, and arm themselves with a staff. The main characteristic of the Wizard is intelligence.

Class bonuses

If the Wizard uses a spell of one element within three moves, then on the third turn, it will deal 100% more magic damage.
